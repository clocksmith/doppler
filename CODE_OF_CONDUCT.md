# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and directly
- focus feedback on technical work, not people
- assume good intent and resolve disagreements with evidence
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community interactions tied to this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or ban contributors for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to project maintainers. Do not post sensitive reports publicly.
